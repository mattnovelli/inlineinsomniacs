☽ inline insomniacs ☽

is a midnight skating club at the Univeristy of Illinois established 2001 by dictators-for-life Joseph Meier and Matthew Harring "to get free Coca-Cola products from the University, and to see if there were any other interested late night skaters on campus."

We continue to meet Wednesdays at midnight at Foellinger Auditorium.
